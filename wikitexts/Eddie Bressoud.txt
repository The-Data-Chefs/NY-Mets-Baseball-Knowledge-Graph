Edward Francis Bressoud (born May 2, 1932) is a former shortstop in Major League Baseball who played from 1956 through 1967 for the New York / San Francisco Giants (1956–1961), Boston Red Sox (1962–1965), New York Mets (1966) and St. Louis Cardinals (1967). He batted and threw right-handed, stood 6 feet 1 inch (1.85 m) tall and weighed 175 pounds (79 kg).
