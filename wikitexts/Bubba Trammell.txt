Thomas Bubba Trammell (born November 6, 1971) is a former Major League Baseball outfielder and designated hitter who played for the Detroit Tigers, Tampa Bay Devil Rays, New York Mets, San Diego Padres, and the New York Yankees.  During his seven Major League seasons, he batted .261 and hit 82 home runs.  Trammell appeared in the 2000 World Series as a member of the Mets.
