James Charles Gosger (born November 6, 1942) is an American former Major League Baseball outfielder and first baseman. He played in the majors for ten seasons between 1963 and 1974 for six different teams. Gosger was listed at 5 feet 11 inches (1.80 m) and 185 pounds (84 kg) and batted and threw left-handed. During his MLB career, Gosger batted .226 with 30 home runs and 411 hits in 705 games played.
Gosger appeared in 555 games at center field (291), left (216) and right (83), and eventually as a first baseman (25).
