Robert Anthony Stock (born November 21, 1989) is an American professional baseball pitcher in the Milwaukee Brewers organization. He has also played in Major League Baseball (MLB) for the San Diego Padres, Boston Red Sox, Chicago Cubs, and New York Mets and in the KBO League for the Doosan Bears. He will pitch for Team Israel in the 2023 World Baseball Classic in Miami, in March 2023.
The St. Louis Cardinals selected Stock in the second round of the 2009 MLB draft. Stock made his MLB debut in 2018 with the Padres. After playing for San Diego in 2019, he played for the Red Sox in 2020 and the Cubs and Mets in 2021, before joining Doosan in the 2022 season. Listed at 6 feet 1 inch (1.85 m) and 214 pounds (97 kg), he throws right-handed and bats left-handed.
