Daniel Joseph Garcia (born April 12, 1980) is a retired Major League Baseball second baseman who played for the New York Mets in 2003 and 2004.
In 2006, with the New York Yankees Triple-A affiliate, the Columbus Clippers, Garcia hit .242, with 3 home runs, and 39 RBI. In 2007, playing for the Somerset Patriots of the independent Atlantic League, Garcia hit .273 and stole 39 bases which led the league. Garcia currently resides in Florida with his family.
